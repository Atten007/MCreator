Методы оттенения окрашивают блок в зависимости от биома.

- **birch_foliage** — <font style="background: #80A755">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font> примеры: minecraft:birch_leaves
- **default_foliage** — <font style="background: #77AB2F">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font> примеры: minecraft:acacia_leaves, minecraft:dark_oak_leaves, minecraft:jungle_leaves, minecraft:mangrove_leaves, minecraft:oak_leaves, minecraft:vines
- **dry_foliage** — <font style="background: #A37546">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font> примеры: minecraft:leaf_litter
- **evergreen_foliage** — <font style="background: #619961">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font> примеры: minecraft:spruce_leaves
- **трава** — <font style="background: #92BC58">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font> примеры: minecraft:fern, minecraft:grass_block (верх), minecraft:large_fern, minecraft:pink_petals (стебли), minecraft:reeds, minecraft:short_grass, minecraft:tall_grass, minecraft:wildflowers (стебли)
- **вода** — <font style="background: #44AFF5">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</font> примеры: minecraft:cauldron (неокрашенная вода), minecraft:flowing_water, minecraft:water
